# Sales-BI-Dashboard


Business Problem

The company lacked a centralized reporting solution to monitor sales performance across products, categories, customers, and countries. Decision-makers struggled to identify sales trends, top-performing products, and growth opportunities.

Dashboard Goal

To provide a single interactive dashboard that tracks key sales KPIs, analyzes product performance, monitors sales trends, and supports data-driven business decisions.

KPI Cards

Total Orders (100)

Measures total transactions.
Helps track business activity and demand.

Total Customers (12)

Shows the number of unique customers.
Helps evaluate customer reach and retention.

Total Sales (103K)

Displays total revenue generated.
Key metric for business performance.
Sales Trend Chart (Line Chart)

Why Used: Best for showing changes over time.

Purpose:

Analyze sales trends by quarter.
Identify growth and seasonal patterns.

Business Insight:

Sales show an overall upward trend with strong growth in 2026.
Product Sales Chart (Horizontal Bar Chart)

Why Used: Ideal for comparing product performance.

Purpose:

Rank products by sales revenue.
Identify top and low-performing products.

Business Insight:

Dell XPS 15 is the highest revenue-generating product.
Product Category Chart (Donut Chart)

Why Used: Shows category contribution to total quantity sold.

Purpose:

Compare product category performance.
Understand product mix.

Business Insight:

Laptops contribute the highest sales volume, while Smart Home products contribute the least.
Filters & Slicers
Order Date: Analyze performance for specific periods.
Country: Compare sales across regions.
Product Category: Focus on specific product segments.
Business Impact
Improves sales monitoring and reporting.
Helps identify top-selling products and categories.
Supports inventory and marketing decisions.
Enables trend analysis and sales forecasting.
Provides faster, data-driven decision-making for management.
Portfolio Description

Developed an interactive Power BI Sales Dashboard to track revenue, orders, customers, product performance, and category trends across multiple countries using KPI cards, DAX measures, and interactive visualizations for business intelligence reporting.
